About

Telling African
stories through
motion & image

I'm Alvin, an animator, illustrator, and graphic designer based in Nairobi, Kenya. My work sits at the intersection of clean visual storytelling and the most African creative tradition: bold shapes, deliberate color, and imagery rooted in the continent's richness.

I was the person behind Hadithi Time with Sifa and Jake, an animated Kenyan children's series I wrote, directed voice overs for, animated, and edited from the ground up. I've worked across animation, editorial illustration, brand identity, and campaign design for NGOs, foundations, and creative businesses, including The Feminist Code, Glow Together Foundation, Live Life Adventures, and Doodle Solutions, building work that holds the eye and earns its space.
